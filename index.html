Who is SNDK?


PT. SIKO NAKAMURA DWI KARYA (SNDK), is Engineering, Fabrication and Construction Company, which is dedicated as a One Stop Services Workshop. We provide high quality of services in Engineering Design, Manufacturing, Fabrication, Installation, Commissioning, and After Sale Maintenance. Our activities are supported by professional and experienced human resources.
